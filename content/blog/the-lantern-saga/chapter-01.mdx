> The lantern had burned every night for thirty years, and the gatekeeper had
> never once asked who it was for.

This is the first chapter of my translation of _The Lantern Saga_. Each chapter
is published as its own article under this novel's topic.

## Translator's note

The original opens with a wordplay between _gate_ and _waiting_ that simply does
not survive a literal rendering. Rather than footnote it to death, I leaned into
the imagery of the lantern to carry the same sense of patient vigilance.

Translation is less about words and more about **what a sentence is doing** —
and then finding an English sentence that does the same thing.
